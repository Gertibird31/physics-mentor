Physics 1 topics

1) Kinematics

Study how objects move using position, displacement, velocity, and acceleration in one and two dimensions.

2) Dynamics and Newton's Laws

Connect changes in motion to forces, including free-body diagrams, friction, tension, and normal forces.

3) Work and Energy

Use work-energy principles to analyze motion, energy transfers, and conservation of mechanical energy.

4) Momentum and Collisions

Model interactions between objects using impulse and conservation of momentum in elastic and inelastic collisions.

5) Circular Motion and Gravitation

Explain centripetal acceleration, circular force requirements, and gravitational attraction in orbital contexts.

6) Rotational Motion (Intro)

Introduce angular kinematics, torque, and rotational equilibrium to extend translational mechanics ideas.
