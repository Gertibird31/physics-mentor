Physics 1 topics and sequence

Download syllabus draft

Topic 1 · Kinematics

Goal: describe motion with position, velocity, and acceleration.

  1. Motion graphs and units
  2. Average vs instantaneous velocity
  3. Acceleration and constant-acceleration models
  4. 2D projectile motion

Topic 2 · Dynamics

Goal: connect motion changes to net force using Newton's laws.

  1. Inertia and Newton's first law
  2. Free-body diagrams
  3. Applying F = ma in one and two dimensions
  4. Friction, tension, and normal force models

Topic 3 · Energy

Goal: solve problems with work-energy methods.

  1. Work and kinetic energy
  2. Potential energy and conservative forces
  3. Energy bar charts and transfer tracking
  4. Power and efficiency

Topic 4 · Momentum

Goal: analyze collisions and interactions in closed systems.

  1. Impulse and momentum change
  2. Conservation of momentum
  3. 1D elastic and inelastic collisions
  4. Center-of-mass reasoning
